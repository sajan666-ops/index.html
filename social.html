SOCIAL HISTORY OF FARIDKOT

Faridkot's population as per the 2011 Census is 617,508, with a sex ratio of 889 females per 1,000 males and a literacy rate of 70.6%. Historically dominated by Jat Sikh and Mazhabi Sikh communities, the district saw a transformation in social dynamics post-independence through land reforms and rural electrification. Today, over 65% of people still reside in villages, but there is a growing trend toward urban lifestyles and inter-caste cooperation, especially in education and health sectors.
