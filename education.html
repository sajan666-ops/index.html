3. HIGHER EDUCATION

(a) Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS)
Location: Faridkot City
Established: 1998
Significance: Sole health sciences university in Punjab
Programs Offered:
MBBS, BDS, B.Sc Nursing, B.Pharmacy, M.Sc (Medical), MD/MS
Affiliated with 150+ institutions across Punjab
Role: Conducts medical entrance exams, manages affiliations, research and regulation of healthcare education

(b) Guru Gobind Singh Medical College and Hospital
Type: Government Medical College under BFUHS
Courses: MBBS, MD/MS, super-specialties
Hospital: Teaching hospital with 1,000+ beds, handling 800+ outpatients daily

(c) Government Brijindra College, Faridkot
Established: 1942 (pre-independence)
Courses: B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., M.A. (Punjabi, History, Political Science), M.Sc. (Math, Physics)
Affiliation: Punjabi University, Patiala

(d) Malwa College of Nursing
Offers diploma and degree programs in General Nursing, Midwifery, and B.Sc Nursing.

5. KEY EDUCATIONAL CHALLENGES

Urban-Rural Divide: Urban students have better access to English medium and CBSE schools
Teacher Shortage: Especially in science, math, and English in govt schools
Female Dropouts: Due to socio-economic issues in rural areas
Digital Divide: Many govt schools lack access to high-speed internet, especially in villages
Career Guidance: Limited career counseling facilities in schools/colleges

6. RECENT REFORMS & DEVELOPMENTS

Smart Schools Project: Over 50 government schools converted into smart schools with digital boards and tablets
E-Punjab School Management System: For attendance, performance tracking
Free Coaching Programs: For NEET, JEE, and other competitive exams in govt colleges
Mission Shat Pratishat: Aimed at 100% pass result in 10th and 12th boards

7. SPECIAL ACHIEVEMENTS

Students from Faridkot have topped medical entrance exams in Punjab.
GGSMCH and BFUHS have produced hundreds of doctors serving across India and abroad.
Government Brijindra College has been awarded for best NSS and cultural activities regionally.
